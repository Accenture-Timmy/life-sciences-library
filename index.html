About this library

A shared reading resource for the Accenture Life Sciences practice team.

How to borrow a book

  • 1Scan the QR code at the bookshelf, or visit this page directly.
  • 2Browse available books and tap the one you'd like to borrow.
  • 3Enter your name, email and choose a return date (up to 4 weeks).
  • 4Take the book from the shelf — you're all set!
  • 5To return, come back here, go to My Loans, search your name and tap Return.

Borrowing guidelines

📅Maximum loan period is 4 weeks. Please return on time so others can borrow.
🔄You can renew a book once for another 2 weeks by returning it in My Loans and re-borrowing.
📖Please take good care of all books — they're a shared resource for the whole team.
➕Have a book to donate? Let Shane or Timmy know and it can be added to the catalogue.
